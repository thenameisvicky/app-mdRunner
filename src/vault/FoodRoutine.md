# 7-Day Morning Meal Plan (₹30/day) — Kettle Only

**Goal:**  

- Cheap (≤ ₹30/day)  
- Low effort  
- Supports running + brain recovery  
- No air fryer (kettle only)

## Weekly Meal Plan

| Day | Meal | How to Prepare (Kettle) | Approx Cost (₹) |
| ---- | ----- | ------------------------- | ---------------- |
| Day 1 | 2 boiled eggs + 1 banana | Boil eggs in kettle (10–12 min) | 20 |
| Day 2 | 1 boiled egg + boiled potato (150g) | Boil potato chunks in kettle | 13 |
| Day 3 | 2 boiled eggs + 1 bread slice | Eggs in kettle, bread plain | 17 |
| Day 4 | 1 boiled egg + 1 banana + small potato | Boil egg & potato together | 17 |
| Day 5 | 2 boiled eggs (salt only) | Kettle boil | 16 |
| Day 6 | Boiled potato (200g) + 1 bread slice | Kettle boil potato | 11 |
| Day 7 | 2 boiled eggs + 1 banana | Kettle boil | 20 |

## Cost Summary

- Weekly total ≈ ₹115–120  
- Daily average ≈ ₹16–18  
- Buffer available for extra egg or milk

## Preparation Notes

- Eggs: place in kettle, cover with water, boil 10–12 min  
- Potatoes: cut small pieces for faster boiling (12–15 min)  
- Add salt after cooking (avoid adding inside kettle)

## Brain & Energy Notes

- Eggs → protein + choline (memory & focus)  
- Potatoes / banana → glucose for thinking & running recovery  
- Consistency > variety

## Important Rules

1. Never skip breakfast completely  
2. Eat within 60–90 minutes of waking  
3. Drink water after run (pinch of salt optional)
