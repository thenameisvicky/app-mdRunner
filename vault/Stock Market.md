## Goal

This roadmap is designed to help you learn the stock market from scratch, develop trading and investing skills, and gradually grow your capital safely. The focus is on **skill development, risk management, and realistic profit goals**, not quick riches.

## Phase 1: Learning the Basics (Month 1–3)

**Objective:** Understand how the stock market works, basic analysis, and risk management.

| Focus Area | Resources / Tools | Notes |
|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Stock Market Fundamentals | Groww Blog, Investopedia | Learn what stocks, ETFs, indices, mutual funds are |
| Financial Statements | “How to Read Financial Statements” by Investopedia | Focus on balance sheet, income statement, cash flow |
| Types of Analysis | Pranjal Kamra YouTube, CA Rachana Ranade YouTube | Fundamental vs technical analysis |
| Risk Management | Books: “The Intelligent Investor” by Benjamin Graham | Learn position sizing, stop-loss, diversification |

## Phase 2: Paper Trading / Simulated Trading (Month 3–6)

**Objective:** Practice trading without risking real money.

| Focus Area | Tools / Resources | Notes |
|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Virtual Trading Platforms | Groww virtual portfolio, Moneybhai, Investopedia Simulator | Track entries, exits, performance, mistakes |
| Strategy Practice | Swing trading, long-term investing, sector rotation | Test strategies with clear rules and record results |
| Journaling & Analysis | Excel / Google Sheets | Record all trades, emotions, and lessons learned |

## Phase 3: Small Real Investments (Month 6–12)

**Objective:** Start investing real money with low risk.

| Focus Area | Capital | Notes |
|------------|--------|------|
| Long-term Investing | ₹5,000–₹10,000 | Invest in fundamentally strong companies or ETFs |
| SIPs in Mutual Funds | ₹2,000–₹5,000/month | Build consistency and discipline |
| Risk Control | Stop-loss, diversification | Avoid over-leveraging and high-risk trades |
| Reflection & Adjustment | Monthly review | Track performance and adapt strategies |

## Phase 4: Advanced Strategies & Market Dynamics (Year 1–3)

**Objective:** Develop deeper understanding and advanced trading skills.

| Focus Area | Tools / Resources | Notes |
|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Macro Trends & Market Sentiment | Economic Times, Bloomberg, Moneycontrol | Learn to interpret interest rates, GDP, sector performance |
| Technical Strategies | TradingView, Zerodha Varsity | Advanced charting, indicators, candlestick patterns |
| Strategy Development | Backtesting with historical data | Create and refine your own trading strategy |
| Continuous Learning | Newsletters, books, courses | Stay updated and adapt to market changes |

## Key Principles

1. **Patience:** Skill development takes months to years.  
2. **Risk Management:** Never invest money you can't afford to lose.  
3. **Consistency:** Focus on building habits, not chasing fast profits.  
4. **Documentation:** Maintain a trading journal to learn from wins and losses.
